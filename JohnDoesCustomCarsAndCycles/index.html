Our Story

John Doe's Custom Cars and Cycles began as a big dream in a little boy’s heart. John Doe spent countless afternoons in the garage with his grandfather, learning how to rebuild old classic cars one bolt at a time. Those early lessons weren’t just about engines; they were about patience, pride, and the joy of creating something with your own hands. What started as simple tinkering grew into a lifelong passion that eventually became the full‑service custom shop we run today. Every build we take on still carries that same spirit: craftsmanship rooted in family, tradition, and the belief that every ride deserves a story worth telling.

From frame‑off restorations to subtle performance upgrades, we balance old‑school mechanical know‑how with modern tools and diagnostics. We listen first to how you drive, where you drive, and what you want your ride to say about you, then we design a build that fits your life.

What we do

The values learned in John Doe's grandfather’s garage still guide our work today. Whether we’re restoring a classic, tuning a modern muscle car, or shaping a custom motorcycle, we approach every project with the same care and curiosity that sparked this dream decades ago. We don’t rush builds or cut corners. We build machines that are meant to last, meant to be driven, meant to thrill, and meant to be remembered.

  • Custom Builds: Engine swaps, suspension upgrades, brake conversions, and drivetrain tuning.
  • Restorations: Rust repair, bodywork, interior refresh, and period‑correct details.
  • Detail and Finishing: Paint correction, ceramic coatings, and show‑prep packages.
  • Motorcycle Customs: Café racers, bobbers, and touring setups tailored to your riding style.

Every project is treated like the ones that started it all — hands‑on, intentional, and built with pride.
